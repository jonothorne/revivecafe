Your Local Cafe In The Heart Of The Community

What started as Revive Coffee Hut has blossomed into Revive Cafe. We've moved from our beloved outdoor hut into a warm, welcoming indoor space where we can serve you better, rain or shine.

Run by Alive Church, all proceeds go towards our work in the local community. Every cup of coffee you enjoy helps us support those who need it most.
