Welcome to Our New Indoor Space!

From coffee hut to community haven - Revive Cafe's exciting new chapter

We're thrilled to announce that Revive Cafe has made the move from our beloved outdoor coffee hut into a warm, welcoming indoor space at Alive House on Nelson Street in Norwich!

A New Space, Same Heart

For those who've been with us since the beginning, you'll remember the humble coffee hut where it all started. Rain or shine, hot or cold, we served our community with a smile. While we loved that outdoor spirit, we knew we could serve you better with a proper indoor space.

Now, located at Alive House on the corner of Nelson Street and Arms Street (opposite The Nelson pub), we have the room to welcome more friends, offer comfortable seating, and create a true community gathering space.

What's New?

  • Indoor seating - No more weather worries! Enjoy your coffee in comfort
  • More space - Room for community gatherings and events
  • Still dog-friendly - Your furry friends are always welcome
  • Extended menu options - More food and drink choices
  • Free WiFi - Perfect for remote work or studying

Our Mission Continues

What hasn't changed? Our commitment to supporting the Norwich community. As always, 100% of our proceeds go toward supporting local people in need through Alive Church's community work.

Every coffee you buy, every sandwich you enjoy, every moment you spend with us contributes to:

  • Emergency support for Norwich families in crisis
  • Safe space and support services
  • Community development initiatives
  • Youth programs and activities
