How Your Coffee Helped Norwich This Year

Reflecting on the real-world impact of our social enterprise cafe

As 2025 comes to a close, we want to share something special with you: the story of how your coffee made a real difference in Norwich this year.

At Revive Cafe, we're not just serving great coffee—we're a social enterprise run by Alive Church, where 100% of proceeds support local people in need. And this year, thanks to you, we've been able to make a real impact.

Your Coffee in Action

Every flat white, every latte, every pastry you purchased at our dog-friendly Norwich cafe has directly funded:

Emergency Support for Families

When local families face unexpected crises—job loss, housing emergencies, or urgent needs—your coffee purchases helped provide:

  • Emergency food packages for families in crisis
  • Essential items for children and families
  • Financial support for urgent household needs
  • Referrals to additional support services

Safe Space & Community Support

Alive House, where we're located on Nelson Street, serves as more than just our cafe—it's a safe space for anyone who needs it. Your support has helped fund:

  • Drop-in support sessions for people facing challenges
  • Mental health and wellbeing support
  • A warm, welcoming environment where everyone belongs
  • Community events bringing Norwich residents together

Youth & Community Programs

We believe in investing in Norwich's future. Your coffee has helped fund:

  • Youth activities and mentoring programs
  • Community development initiatives
  • Skills training and support for local residents
  • Events that strengthen community connections

Real Stories, Real Impact

"I never expected that buying my morning coffee could help someone in my own community. Knowing that every purchase at Revive Cafe supports real people in Norwich makes it more than just a cafe—it's a way to give back while getting great coffee."

— Sarah, Regular Customer

The Difference You Make

Here's what makes our social enterprise model unique: we're not asking for donations. You're simply enjoying great coffee in a welcoming, dog-friendly space. The social good happens naturally, built into every transaction.

When you choose Revive Cafe over other Norwich cafes, you're choosing to invest in your community. You're saying that business can be a force for good, and that profit can have purpose.

Looking Ahead to 2026

With our new indoor space at Alive House, we have even more opportunities to serve the Norwich community. We're planning:

  • More community events and gatherings
  • Expanded support services for local residents
  • Partnerships with other Norwich organizations
  • More ways your coffee can make a difference

Thank You

To every customer who's chosen Revive Cafe this year—whether you're a daily regular or you've visited once—thank you. Your support makes this work possible.

You're not just buying coffee. You're funding emergency food for families. You're providing a safe space for someone in crisis. You're investing in Norwich's youth. You're building community.

That's the power of a social enterprise cafe. That's the difference you make. That's why we do what we do.
